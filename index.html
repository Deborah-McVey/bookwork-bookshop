About The Bookworm Bookshop

Since 1933, The Bookwork Bookshop has offered rare and hard-to-find titles for the discerning reader. The Bookworm Bookshop offers:

  • Friendly, knowledgeable, and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try before you buy"
  • Four cuddly cats: Esmerelda, Catherine, Dulcinea, and Beatrice

The Bookworm Bookshop hours are 10am to 9pm on Tuesday through Friday, noon to 7pm on Saturday and Sunday, and closed on Monday.

Upcoming Events
